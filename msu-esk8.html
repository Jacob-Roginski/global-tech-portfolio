MSU E-Skateboard Club

President & Designer | Ongoing

Overview

As President and Designer of the MSU E-Skateboard Club, I lead both the organizational operations and the visual identity of the club. This ongoing project combines leadership, community building, and design to create an engaging experience for electric skateboard enthusiasts on campus.

The Challenge

Building a thriving student organization from the ground up required addressing multiple challenges:

  • Establishing a recognizable brand identity for the club
  • Creating engaging content to attract and retain members
  • Designing promotional materials and digital presence
  • Organizing events and maintaining community engagement

My Approach

Brand Development

Created a cohesive visual identity including logo, color palette, and brand guidelines.

Community Building

Organized regular group rides, events, and workshops to foster community engagement.

Digital Presence

Designed and maintained social media content and promotional materials.

Leadership

Coordinated with university administration and managed club operations and member relations.

Key Features

Visual Identity

Comprehensive brand system including logo, typography, and color schemes for club materials.

Event Organization

Regular group rides, safety workshops, and community events for club members.

Social Media

Engaging content strategy and design for digital platforms to grow membership.

Safety Education

Educational materials and workshops promoting safe e-skateboard practices.

Outcome & Learnings

Leading the MSU E-Skateboard Club has been an incredible opportunity to combine my passion for electric skateboarding with my design and leadership skills. The club has grown into a vibrant community of enthusiasts who share knowledge, organize rides, and promote safe e-skateboard practices on campus.

Key learnings include the importance of consistent branding, community engagement, and balancing creative vision with practical organizational needs. This experience has strengthened my ability to lead teams, manage projects, and create cohesive visual identities.
