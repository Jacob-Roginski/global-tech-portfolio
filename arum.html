Arum

UI/UX Designer & Product Manager | Fall 2025

Overview

Arum is a comprehensive digital solution designed to streamline workflows and enhance user productivity. This project showcases the intersection of intuitive design and strategic product management, focusing on creating a seamless experience for diverse user needs.

The Challenge

Users needed a unified platform that could adapt to their workflow without adding complexity. The main challenges were:

  • Balancing feature richness with simplicity and ease of use
  • Creating an intuitive interface for users with varying technical backgrounds
  • Ensuring seamless integration with existing tools and workflows
  • Maintaining performance and reliability at scale

My Approach

User-Centered Design

Conducted extensive user interviews and usability studies to understand pain points and needs.

Rapid Prototyping

Created and iterated on prototypes to validate design concepts and gather early feedback.

Design System

Developed a comprehensive design system to ensure consistency and scalability.

Product Strategy

Prioritized features based on user impact and business value, managing the product roadmap.

Key Features

Intelligent Workflow

Smart automation and suggestions that adapt to user behavior and preferences.

Unified Interface

Clean, consistent design language that reduces cognitive load and improves efficiency.

Seamless Integration

Easy connection with popular tools and services users already rely on.

Real-Time Collaboration

Built-in features for team communication and collaborative work.

Outcome & Learnings

This project demonstrated the power of combining user-centered design with strategic product thinking. By maintaining a close feedback loop with users throughout development, we created a solution that truly addresses their needs while maintaining technical feasibility.

Key learnings included the importance of prioritization in product development, the value of design systems for maintaining consistency, and how effective communication between design and engineering teams leads to better outcomes.
