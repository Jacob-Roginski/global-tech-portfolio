Capital Area Housing Partnership

UI/UX Designer & Product Manager | Spring 2025

Overview

A comprehensive digital platform designed to connect individuals and families with affordable housing resources in the Capital Area. This project demonstrates the power of design to create social impact, focusing on accessibility, usability, and empathy-driven solutions.

The Challenge

Finding affordable housing is a complex and often overwhelming process. The main challenges were:

  • Creating an accessible interface for users with varying digital literacy levels
  • Simplifying complex housing information and digestible processes
  • Building trust and transparency in the housing search experience
  • Ensuring navigation accessibility for intuitive design

My Approach

Empathy Research

Conducted interviews with housing seekers to understand their challenges and needs firsthand.

Accessibility First

Designed with WCAG standards and clear language to ensure inclusivity for all users.

Simplified Workflows

Streamlined complex processes into clear, manageable steps with progress indicators.

Stakeholder Collaboration

Worked closely with housing experts and community partners to validate solutions.

Key Features

Resource Finder

Intuitive search and filtering system to help users find relevant housing resources quickly.

Application Assistant

Guided application process with clear instructions and document checklists.

Mobile-Optimized

Fully responsive design ensuring access for users on smartphones and tablets.

Educational Content

Plain-language guides and resources to help users understand housing options.

Outcome & Learnings

This project reinforced the importance of designing with empathy and understanding the real-world context of users. By focusing on accessibility and simplicity, we created a platform that empowers individuals to navigate the housing search process with confidence.

Key learnings included the value of plain language in design, the importance of mobile-first thinking for underserved populations, and how collaboration with community partners leads to more impactful solutions that truly serve user needs.
